The Rover

Bogie-6 Autonomous Logistics Platform

STAGED: AUGUST 2026

WORK IN PROGRESS

Currently in R&D Lifecycle | Expected Completion: AUGUST 2026

Proprietary Locomotion

The Rover utilizes a high-torque **6-Wheel Bogie Suspension** system, enabling it to navigate high-impedance construction environments including loose rubble, rebar mesh, and 30-degree inclines.

Autonomous Logic Cluster

Driven by an NVIDIA Jetson Nano parallel-processing unit. The system runs real-time SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) to operate in GPS-denied environments like underground parking structures or internal shafts.

Modular Toolhead

The August 2026 update introduces the **Vortex-1 Asphalt Manipulator**, allowing for automated precision layering on urban road projects with sub-centimeter accuracy.
