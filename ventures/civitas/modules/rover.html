The Rover

DESIGN COMPLETE // READY

Mission Profile

The Civitas Rover is engineered to handle the "Three Ds" of construction: Dull, Dirty, and Dangerous tasks. It is not just a vehicle; it is a modular platform.

Built on a 6-wheel independent Bogie suspension, it can traverse rebar grids, mud, and rubble. Its primary tool-head is designed for **Automated Asphalt Layering**, but it can be reconfigured for brick transport or hazardous gas inspections.

Autonomous Logic

Powered by a Jetson Nano cluster, the Rover uses LIDAR and Stereo Vision to navigate dynamic sites without GPS. It identifies workers and stops instantly to prevent collisions.
